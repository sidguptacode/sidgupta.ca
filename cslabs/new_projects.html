ML research & projects
I hope to continue research in machine learning, interpretability, and computer vision for healthcare. To date, my research and projects encompass:

1. Combining multiple modalities of data to emulate clinical predictions

2. Making black-box ML models more interpretable to understand

3. Applying ML algorithms in real-world settings to improve health in an accessible way

Personal ML projects

Aphrodite Data Science Lead

Aphrodite is a university-wide matchmaking service that to date has matched over 40,000 students. I work with a talented group of volunteers to:

1. Apply ML on a student dataset to gain insights on relationship science and improve matching

2. Coordinate matching of students using a robust matching algorithm

3. Develop insightful data reports that build community and highlight similarities among groups of people

Team: https://aphrodite.global/about

Explaining eye-tracking CNNs

We apply DeepDream and SmoothGrad on CNNs for eye-tracking, and explain some of their black-box behavior

Team: Sid Gupta* , David Canagasabey* , Melissa Halim Lee*

Visualizing Fairness in ML

We visualize latent embeddings and weights of different ML models on an unfair dataset, comparing fair generative models, debiasing models, and unfair models

Team: Sid Gupta* , Tina Li* , Melissa Hu*

CoughMotion
